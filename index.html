About Us

The Tipsy Pixies are a new breed of entertainer for hire. We accompany you and your friends around the event of your choosing. We play, dance, and interact with you to pump you up and enhance your experience. We'll dress in theme and carry toys for your amusement, a sound system for your enjoyment, a camera for your documentation, and we'll pull out the lights when it gets dark.

We believe in creating moments of magic for you at all your favorite events and excel at giving you the experiences you'll never forget. We bring the love with the energy and value connection, fun, and social adventure, so what are you waiting for?
